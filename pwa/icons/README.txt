Add PWA icons here so "Add to Home Screen" shows your app icon:

  - icon-192x192.png   (192 x 192 pixels)
  - icon-512x512.png   (512 x 512 pixels)

You can resize assets/mte_logo.png to these sizes (e.g. with an image editor or online tool).
The app and reverse proxy must serve these at /icons/icon-192x192.png and /icons/icon-512x512.png.
