Current State:
- ALL PHASES COMPLETE: All 6 development phases from roadmap.txt are fully implemented and functional. The system is production-ready with a complete end-to-end workflow.

- Core Infrastructure (Phases 1-3):
  * Database: SQLite with WAL mode, foreign keys, soft deletes. Tables: users, vendors, cars, requisitions with full schema including pricing, status tracking, serial numbers, timestamps.
  * Authentication: bcrypt password hashing, secure login/logout, session management, role-based access control.
  * Application Shell: Streamlit app with role-based navigation, sidebar menu, notification badges for pending approvals, custom styling system.

- Master Data Module:
  * Vendors: Full CRUD with soft delete/reactivate, address field, company association.
  * Vehicles: Full CRUD with soft delete/reactivate, fuel_type (Diesel/Unleaded/Premium), company field (MTEI/DSRDC/DIC/GCEIC/MTEI Trucking), default vendor, driver_name, plate number validation.

- Requests Module:
  * Request submission with auto-calc pricing, FULLTANK option, fuel type from vehicle, auto-select vendor from vehicle.
  * Request history with lazy loading, edit functionality (one-time edit, creator or higher role only, pending status only).
  * Dynamic company logo display based on selected vehicle.
  * Confirmation dialogs for submissions.

- Approvals Module:
  * Approver/Accounting role can view pending requests, approve/reject with confirmation dialogs.
  * Edit functionality for pending requests, FULLTANK to numeric conversion support.
  * Icon-based UI (✏️ Edit, ✓ Approve, ✗ Reject), compact button layout.

- Purchasing Module:
  * PO generation workflow with unit price encoding (defaults to current vendor fuel price by fuel type), PDF download capability.
  * Once per session, purchaser is prompted \"Do you have fuel price updates?\" If Yes, dialog opens with one tab per vendor; each tab has Diesel/Unleaded/Premium (₱/liter) and Update button. Table vendor_fuel_prices stores per-vendor prices.
  * Receiving workflow to mark POs as received/delivered.
  * Download icon (📥) for PO PDFs, pricing display throughout.

- Billing Module:
  * Checkbox-based batch selection for received items.
  * Invoice number capture and reconciliation.
  * Status update to 'Billed' with invoice tracking.

- Reports Module:
  * Status-based summaries (Pending, Approved, PO Generated, Received, Billed).
  * Cost totals and grand totals per status group.
  * Billed items sorted by invoice number.

- Dashboard Module (Finance Role Only):
  * Month/year selector for analytics.
  * Pie chart visualization: fuel usage by vehicle or by company (toggle).
  * Top 10 consuming vehicles per company with total liters and request count.
  * FULLTANK requests list grouped by company.
  * Fuel price trend graph: monthly average prices for last 12 months, separate lines per fuel type (Diesel/Unleaded/Premium), optional vendor filter, price statistics (latest/min/max).

- User Management Module (Finance Role Only):
  * Full CRUD: create, edit, deactivate, reactivate users.
  * Required full_name field for PO printouts (displays in Prepared By/Approved By).
  * Role assignment (User, Approver, Purchaser, Accounting).

- PDF Generation:
  * PO printout with company branding (MTE and Planters logos), centered company info, serial numbers, pricing, fuel type, prepared_by/approved_by full names, fallback logo handling.
  * HTML-to-PDF via jinja2 + xhtml2pdf.

- Additional Features:

  * Fuel type tracking and validation.
